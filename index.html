Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation sparks progress, challenges norms, embraces creativity, and transforms ideas into impactful, real-world solutions.

Sustainability

Sustainability focuses on living and developing in a way that balances environmental care, social well-being, and economic growth.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure is the foundation of a society, including roads, bridges, energy, and communication systems, that supports daily life and economic growth.

Empowerment

Empowerment happens when people gain the confidence and resources to take control of their own choices, leading to personal and collective growth.

The Vision Of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation's trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Advancing solar and wind energy initiatives to promote sustainability and global environmental leadership.

Modern Infrastructure

Developing advanced infrastructure to drive economic growth and enhance global competitiveness
